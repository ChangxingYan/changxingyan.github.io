ECNU Baseball & Softball Society Visual Identity System

The design of Visual Identity System for ECNU Baseball and Softball Society. Logo design for both Baseball Team and Softball Team, trying to unite the two Teams with one visual style but also offering differentiation. Keeping the design of original jerseys and cap design, I adjusted and added new design into it.

  • — category: graphic design
  • — year: 2019

Being a member of B&S society myself, I chose it as my subject of my final project of CI design. Our original jersey was a rip-off of good ol' Detroit Tigers', probably a special edition of St. Patrick Day. Most of the designs are accumulated along the way, done by many generations of creative students, since it has been thirteen years since the team established.

I would like to keep the original design but also keep them in line and in fashion, united within a intact visual identity system. The most difficult part was decide the color palette for the team, to decide whether to keep the remarkable St. Patrick green or not. Finally, the dark green became a sub-color and a brighter green which is the color of softball comes up into place.
